About Vergeside Pickups

Vergeside pickups (also known as bulk rubbish collection) are when your local council collects large household items from the front of your property.

This service is typically offered 1-4 times per year depending on your local council. This website helps you find out when your next collection is scheduled.

How to Use This Service

1. Find Your Area

Use the map on the home page to locate your suburb or council area.

2. Check Collection Dates

See when your area's next vergeside pickup is scheduled by looking at the color-coded map.

3. Plan Accordingly

Prepare your items for collection according to your council's guidelines.

About This Website

This website was created to provide a simple way for residents to check when their vergeside pickup is scheduled. The information is gathered from local council websites and updated regularly.

If you find any incorrect information or would like to suggest improvements, please contact us.
