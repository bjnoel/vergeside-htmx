Our Mission

Vergeside.com.au aims to be the most comprehensive resource for finding bulk waste collection information across WA. We help residents easily discover when their next vergeside pickup is scheduled.

Our user-friendly map interface shows you exactly when collections are happening in your area, making it simple to plan ahead and participate in this important waste disposal service.

What Are Vergeside Collections?

Vergeside collections (also known as bulk rubbish collections, hard waste, or kerbside collections) are special waste collection services provided by local councils where residents can place large household items on the verge in front of their property for collection.

These collections allow residents to dispose of items too large for regular bin collection, such as furniture, appliances, and garden waste. The service frequency varies by council, usually occurring between 1-4 times per year.

Why I Created This Service

This site was created to improve upon an existing vergeside pickup map that was created by Matt McGlew in 2011. The map proved to be very popular, and at the time of writing has been viewed over 500,000 times. However, I saw an opportunity to improve upon this map in couple of ways:

  • There was no search by date functionality in the map
  • It was difficult to update the map with new information

The platform centralizes this information and presents it visually, making it much easier for residents to plan their waste disposal efficiently.

Data Accuracy

The pickup dates and pickup areas has been pulled from individual council websites as well as from Nearmaps.

However, councils may occasionally change collection schedules with limited notice. We always recommend verifying the information with your local council if you're planning a major cleanup.

If you notice any discrepancies or have more recent information, please let us know so we can update our database.
